Biryani Receipe

It is a very delicious dish primarily for non-veg eaters.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Palak
  • Rice
  • Masala

Instructions

  1. Marinate the chicken first.
  2. Paste the palak and add it to chicken.
  3. Add rice to boiling water with above chicken paste.
  4. Add masala and cook for 15 minutes.
